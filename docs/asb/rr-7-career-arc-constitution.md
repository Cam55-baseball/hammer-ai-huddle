# RR-7 — Career Arc & Longitudinal Identity Constitution

**Status:** Sealed (Post-Mastery doctrine phase). Doctrine only.
**Scope:** the `relational.career_arc.*` topic family and any future
longitudinal milestone, developmental arc, or transition memory
surfaces derived from it.
**Subordinate to:** Eternal Laws, Megaphase 151–160, RR-1…RR-6, the
developmental gating matrix, the relational visibility matrix, and all
prior immutable invariants across Phases 1–150.

Career arcs are replay-derived summaries of who an athlete has been
becoming. They are never predictions, never destinies, never identities.

## RR-7 invariants

1. **No deterministic destiny framing.** No surface may claim a future
   level, role, scholarship, signing, draft outcome, or career
   trajectory as inevitable, probable, or "on track." Confidence and
   missingness remain visible.
2. **No future guarantees.** Projection surfaces may describe ranges,
   bounded scenarios (per SF-1…SF-10 forecasting invariants), and
   observable trends, never guarantees.
3. **No identity locking.** The athlete is never reduced to a label
   ("a pitcher," "a slugger," "a recruit"). Roles are observed and may
   be redirected at any time.
4. **Athlete may redefine direction at any time.** A
   `relational.career_arc.goal_stated` event from the athlete is
   constitutional. It supersedes any inferred arc immediately on the
   next projection rebuild.
5. **Setbacks remain part of continuity without defining identity.**
   Injuries (RR-6), slumps (RR-5), pauses, and exits remain in the
   ledger and remain visible. They do not collapse into identity
   labels or destiny framing.
6. **Career arcs are replay-derived summaries only.** They never
   author `organism_truth`, `athlete_intent`, `authority_override`,
   `hard_stop`, or `rehabilitation_state`. They never gate execution.
7. **The organism adapts continuously.** Arc projections recompute on
   every ledger change. Prior arc snapshots remain available for
   replay; they are not promoted to current authority.
8. **Commercial pressure may not shape the arc.** Recruiter visibility,
   monetization incentive, showcase economics, sponsorship pressure,
   and NIL framing may never bias arc summaries (extends RW-1,
   ER-1…ER-10).
9. **Minor-athlete supremacy applies.** For minors, parent visibility
   (per RR-4 invariant 5) governs which arc surfaces are externally
   shareable. Recruiter-visible arcs require parent authorization.
10. **Divergence is first-class.** Branching, redirecting, and exiting
    are observable arc transitions, not failures. Surfaces must
    represent divergence with the same fidelity as continuity.

## Allowable milestone modeling

- Citation-bound retrospectives of past transitions, achievements, and
  developmental gate crossings.
- Bounded forward scenarios with explicit confidence and missingness.
- Athlete-stated goals recorded as canonical events and surfaced
  verbatim with attribution.

## Prohibited predictive framing

- "You will reach X."
- "On track to Y."
- "Destined for Z."
- Hidden ranking, percentile, or "ceiling" scores that imply destiny.
- Comparative arc collapsing ("you're the next ____").
- Identity-pressure framing ("real athletes do ____").

## Longitudinal continuity ethics

- Continuity is descriptive, not prescriptive.
- The athlete owns redirection; the system observes it.
- Setbacks compose with successes without erasure or smoothing.
