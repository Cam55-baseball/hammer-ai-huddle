# RR-8 — Life Context Integration Constitution

**Status:** Sealed (Post-Mastery doctrine phase). Doctrine only.
**Scope:** the `relational.life_context.*` topic family and any future
academic, social, family, or non-sport organism-load surfaces derived
from it.
**Subordinate to:** Eternal Laws, Megaphase 151–160, RR-1…RR-7, the
safeguarding orchestration sub-route, the relational visibility
matrix, and all prior immutable invariants across Phases 1–150.

Life context informs how the organism adapts to non-sport load. It is
not a surveillance surface, not a profile, not an identity.

## RR-8 invariants

1. **Life context never becomes surveillance.** No surface may
   passively infer, scrape, ingest, or persist life context beyond
   what the athlete (or authorized parent for minors) has explicitly
   disclosed via a canonical event.
2. **User retains disclosure control.** Every disclosed life-context
   event is revocable. Revocation removes downstream visibility on the
   next projection rebuild — no cache, no grace period.
3. **Missingness is acceptable.** Absent life-context data is a
   first-class state. No projection may default missingness to
   assumed values, infer probable context, or penalize absence.
4. **No coercive disclosure.** Surfaces may not gate features behind
   life-context disclosure, may not nudge with engagement loops, and
   may not imply disclosure is required for safety or quality of
   recommendations.
5. **Life load can influence recommendations but not identity.**
   Disclosed context may legitimately tighten load ceilings, soften
   schedule pressure, or trigger safeguarding routes. It may never
   become a label or score that travels with the athlete.
6. **Context remains replay-visible and reversible.** Every
   recommendation influenced by life context exposes the contributing
   antecedent one interaction away.
7. **No hidden profiling.** Composite "life stress" or "support"
   scores are forbidden unless every contributing antecedent and its
   confidence/missingness state is lineage-decomposable.
8. **Safeguarding routes preempt visibility.** When disclosed context
   triggers safeguarding (abuse indicators, neglect indicators,
   coercion, self-harm indicators), the safeguarding sub-route runs
   first; the original event may be redacted in non-safeguarding
   projections per the visibility matrix.
9. **Minor-athlete supremacy applies.** For minors, parent visibility
   governs which life-context disclosures are coach- or
   recruiter-visible. Default scope is `self` or `parent` only.
10. **Life context never authors organism truth.** No life-context
    event may set `organism_truth`, `athlete_intent`,
    `authority_override`, `hard_stop`, or `rehabilitation_state`. It
    contributes lineage to bounded recommendations only.

## Allowable context adaptation

- Athlete-disclosed academic load tightening training ceilings.
- Athlete-disclosed travel, sleep disruption, or schedule shift
  softening scheduled volume.
- Parent-disclosed family events triggering safeguarding-aware
  notification posture.
- Coach-visible context (only with athlete or parent authorization).

## Prohibited behavioral profiling

- Inferred socioeconomic, demographic, or household composition state.
- Sentiment or "wellbeing" scoring without lineage decomposition.
- Persistent personality labels.
- Composite "support network strength" surfaces.
- Sharing life context with recruiters under any conditions other than
  explicit, revocable, lineage-visible authorization from the athlete
  (and parent for minors).

## Safeguarding interaction rules

- Disclosed safeguarding-relevant context routes through the
  Megaphase 151–160 safeguarding sub-route first.
- Notification fan-out follows the safeguarding role binding (parent
  for minors, designated safeguarding role for adults where assigned).
- The original disclosure remains in the ledger; downstream
  visibility narrows per the visibility matrix.
