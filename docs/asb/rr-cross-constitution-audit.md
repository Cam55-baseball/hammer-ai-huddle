## Cross-cutting resolutions

- **Authority hierarchy preserved.** Safeguarding > parent (for minors)
  > clinician (for RR-6) > athlete > coach > recruiter > commercial.
  No RR collision inverts this.
- **Replay determinism preserved.** Every RR-5…RR-10 invariant is
  replay-reconstructable at pinned `engine_version` +
  `reasoning_version`.
- **No hidden organism authority.** RR-5, RR-6, RR-7, RR-8, RR-9,
  RR-10 each forbid authoring of `organism_truth` / `athlete_intent` /
  `authority_override` / `hard_stop` / `rehabilitation_state`.
- **Commercial subordination is total.** RR-10 invariant 1 + RR-6
  invariant 4 + RR-9 invariants 1–4 + RW-1…RW-10 commercial governance
  collectively bind commercial pressure under safeguarding.
- **Identity locking is closed.** RR-5 invariant 9 + RR-7 invariants 1–
  3 + RR-7 invariant 5 close every identity-collapse vector.
- **Non-determinism is closed.** All RR-5…RR-10 surfaces inherit
  Megaphase 56 RE-1…RE-10 replay equivalence and Megaphase 76–90 fabric
  determinism.
